NKS Best-Buy Intraday Futures Backtest

For each shortlisted stock from the NKS momentum list, we take the front-month equity futures price at 09:45 IST on the given date and measure how the contract moved at three intraday checkpoints — 12:30, 14:00 and 15:15 IST. The “Best slot” column is whichever of those three times produced the largest absolute price move vs 09:45, and “PnL pts” is the signed points move for that best slot. Source = FUT when the front-month contract is resolvable, else EQ (underlying cash price — intraday future tracks cash 1:1 within the session).
