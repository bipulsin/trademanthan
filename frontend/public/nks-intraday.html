For each shortlisted NKS stock, the simulation waits for the classical 15-minute opening-range breakout to confirm, then takes a long entry at 10:15 IST priced at the session VWAP on the front-month future, sized at the current FUT lot size. Three professional-grade gates decide whether the trade is TAKEN or SKIPPED:

Gate 1 · VWAP entry. Entry price = Σ((H+L+C)/3 · V) / Σ(V) cumulated over 09:15 → 10:15 candles.
Gate 2 · Risk cap. If (VWAP entry − post-10:15 intraday low) × lot size > ₹10,000, the trade is skipped.
Gate 3 · ORB confirmation. The 15-minute opening range high (max high 09:15 → 09:30) must still be holding at 10:15 IST — i.e. the 10:15 candle opens ≥ ORH, confirming the breakout didn't fade during the first hour. Fail ⇒ skipped.
