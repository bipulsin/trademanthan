Help to Setup

  1. Click the Configure button on top right corner of the Cumulative Average Reversal page.
  2. Enter the stock name & its current/bought price and submit.
  3. If you want to upload multiple stocks at one go, please do so by upload file option.
  4. Before upload file, download the sample CSV file, update with your stock data, save it and then upload it.
  5. Now configuring your data is complete, close the popup by clicking outside.
  6. Click the Run Analysis button to get the CAR recommendation.
