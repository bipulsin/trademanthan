1. Introduction

TradeWithCTO ("we," "our," or "us") operates this platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered algorithmic trading platform at www.tradewithcto.com.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google ID, email address, name, and profile picture.
  • Trading Data: Strategy configurations, scan results, broker connections, and trade-related data you create or access through our platform.
  • Broker Data: If you connect a broker (e.g., Upstox, Dhan), we may receive and store instrument data, positions, and related information necessary for your trading activities.
  • Usage Data: Logs of your interactions with our platform, including IP address, browser type, and timestamps.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our trading platform

  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For sign-in. Google's privacy policy applies to data you share with Google: Google Privacy Policy
  • Broker APIs: Upstox, Dhan, or other brokers you connect. Their data practices apply to broker-specific data.
  • Hosting: Our services are hosted on cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS EC2).

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to store your authentication token and preferences. This enables you to stay signed in across sessions. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS). We do not store your broker passwords; we use OAuth tokens where supported.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account and trading data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.
